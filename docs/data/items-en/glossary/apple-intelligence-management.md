## Definition

Apple Intelligence Management refers to the MDM controls introduced in iOS 18+ and macOS 15+ to manage generative AI capabilities. Organizations can restrict whether AI tools can analyze user content (such as emails or documents) or transmission of data to **Private Cloud Compute (PCC)**. This ensures that sensitive institutional data is not inadvertently processed or learned by AI models, maintaining strict data privacy compliance.
