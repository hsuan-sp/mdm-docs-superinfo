## Definition

Apple Pencil is an active stylus for iPad that supports pressure sensitivity and palm rejection. In specialized assessment or classroom scenarios, MDM can be used to disable the Bluetooth connection to the stylus or restrict certain gestures (such as "Double-Tap to switch tools") to prevent distraction or accidental input during strict exams.
