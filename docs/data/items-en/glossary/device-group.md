## Definition

A Device Group is a container in MDM used to categorize and manage hardware. Groups are divided into "Static Groups," where administrators manually select devices to join, and "Smart Groups," which automatically filter devices based on specific criteria (such as iOS version, department, or serial number). Groups are the primary target for deploying management policies and configuration profiles.
