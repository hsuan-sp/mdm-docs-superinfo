## Definition

User-Initiated Enrollment (UIE) is a management onboarding process where a user manually triggers the enrollment of their device by visiting a specific URL or scanning a QR code. Unlike **Automated Device Enrollment (ADE)**, UIE does not provide a full "Supervised" status on its own, and the user has the ability to remove the MDM profile at any time. For this reason, it is generally not recommended for school-owned, one-to-one device programs.

## Plain English

This is a "Voluntary Enrollment Plan." Instead of the school "drafting" the iPad into service automatically (the ADE method), the student or teacher chooses to go to a website and sign up for school management themselves.
