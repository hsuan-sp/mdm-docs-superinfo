## Definition

Schoolwork is a powerful Apple app designed to help K-12 teachers and students stay organized and collaborate effectively. Teachers can use it to share "Handouts" (assignments), assign specific activities within educational apps, and track student progress in real-time. It integrates deeply with Apple Classroom and provides insights into how much time students spend on specific learning tasks.
